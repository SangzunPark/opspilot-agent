
# Escalation Matrix

Escalation is required when the issue has high business impact, legal risk, cancellation risk, enterprise customer impact, or unresolved repeated complaints.

Billing disputes should be escalated when the customer is premium or enterprise and the issue involves cancellation risk, contract risk, or large invoice amounts.

Technical outages should be escalated when the customer is enterprise, multiple users are affected, or the service is unavailable.

Refund requests should be escalated when the amount is high, the customer is premium or enterprise, or the reason is not clearly covered by policy.

If the AI system has low confidence, missing customer information, or no relevant policy source, it should recommend human review.
