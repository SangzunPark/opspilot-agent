
# Technical Outage Guide

A technical outage should be treated as urgent when the customer reports that the service is unavailable, business-critical workflows are blocked, or multiple users are affected.

Enterprise customer outages should be treated as critical by default.

The operations team should check whether the issue is isolated to one user, one customer account, one region, or the entire service.

Recommended next steps include checking system status, collecting affected user information, identifying the start time of the issue, and escalating to the engineering on-call team when business impact is high.

The response should clearly acknowledge the impact and avoid making an exact resolution promise unless confirmed by engineering.
