# Refund Policy

Refund requests should be classified based on amount, customer tier, reason, and contract terms.

Small refund requests from standard customers may be handled through the normal support process.

Refund requests from premium or enterprise customers should be reviewed more carefully, especially when the customer mentions dissatisfaction, renewal risk, or cancellation.

The operations team should not approve a refund automatically unless the refund reason matches an existing policy.

Recommended next steps include collecting invoice details, checking eligibility, reviewing contract terms, and escalating when the refund amount is high or the customer is high-value.
