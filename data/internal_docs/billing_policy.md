# Billing Policy

Billing disputes should be reviewed carefully before making any promise to the customer.

Common billing issues include incorrect invoice amount, duplicate charges, missing discounts, unexpected renewal fees, and tax calculation problems.

For billing disputes, the operations team should first verify the invoice ID, customer account status, contract terms, applied discounts, and recent subscription changes.

If the customer is a premium or enterprise customer, the issue should be handled with higher priority because billing problems may create renewal or cancellation risk.

The recommended first response is to acknowledge the issue, confirm that the billing details will be reviewed, and avoid admitting fault before verification.

If the dispute appears valid, the case should be escalated to the billing operations team.
