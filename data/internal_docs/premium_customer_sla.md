# Premium Customer SLA

Premium customers require faster response times than standard customers.

For high-priority operational issues, the first response should be sent within 4 business hours.

If a premium customer mentions cancellation, contract risk, legal concern, or repeated dissatisfaction, the issue should be escalated to a human manager.

Premium customer issues related to billing, service outage, or account access should not be handled as low priority.

If the system is uncertain about the correct action, it should recommend human escalation instead of giving a risky automated recommendation.
